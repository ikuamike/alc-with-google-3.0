Lioness

African lions are the most social of all big cats and live together in groups or “prides.” A pride consists of about 15 lions. ... Male lions defend the pride's territory while females do most of the hunting.

  • Average Length: 1.4 - 1.8m
  • Average Lifespan: 15 years
